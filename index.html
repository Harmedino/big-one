About Our Company

The National Crown Project is a timely concept aimed at rewarding and mirroring leadership models in the girl child. It is also packaged to inculcate leadership values in the girl child through a process of personal creative solution deployments for community enhacment and development.

 

This process is aimed at fulfilling the major eight Millennium Development Goals (MDGs) which are measures to monitor progress adopted by a consensus of experts from the United Nations Secretariat and IMF, OECD and the World Bank.

    They include:
  • Eradicate extreme poverty and hunger.
  • Achieve universal primary education.
  • Promote gender equality and empower women.
  • Reduce child mortality.
  • Improve maternal health.

The National Crown Project

The National Crown Project is a great opportunity for young girls to shine as take on mantle of leadership from role models.

Applying for the form online is convenient and accessible for many young ladies within the age of 18 - 27.

Empowering Projects

  • Choose a cause from the United Nations sustainability programs.

Recognition and Support

  • Every participant is a winner.
  • After a voting session, the top 30 will be chosen.
  • The lucky winners become immediate UN ambassadors.
  • They represent 30 Governors' wives or female ministers.
  • Each receives support ranging from N5m to 10m for their individual pet projects.
